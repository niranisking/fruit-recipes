Piña Colada

The Piña Colada is a blend of pineapple juice, ice, and coconut cream. The pineapple is known for its health benefits, particularly its high levels of vitamin C and Manganese. While the coconut is made up of healthy fatty acids that your body needs. It is the perfect recipe for a healthy and more rejuvenated complexion due to the presence of bromelain in the pineapple fruit.
